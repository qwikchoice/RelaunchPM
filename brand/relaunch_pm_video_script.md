## TRUST SIGNAL CHECKLIST

Used in script:
- [x] Specific numbers: 72 hours, 48 hours, 18 months, 3 stories, 2-3 things, 22 files
- [x] Named outputs: Week 1 plan, one-page brief, story document - not vague "results"
- [x] Anti-cheerleading: "I'm not going to tell you you've got this"
- [x] Acknowledges the real pain moment before the product
- [x] Shows actual product UI (screen recording of real chat)
- [x] Shows actual output document (not mockup)
- [x] Free + open source = no financial risk to try
- [x] Coach pushes back in the demo (asks about runway, not "how are you feeling")
- [x] No testimonials needed - the product demo IS the proof

## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR HEYGEN

**Avatar selection:**
- Choose a male or female avatar that reads as "someone who has been through it" - not polished corporate, not young influencer
- Business casual, neutral background
- Eye contact must feel direct, not soft

**Voice tone:**
- Measured. Slight pauses. Not energetic.
- The silence at 0:00 and the pause at 0:58 are intentional - do not cut them
- Avoid upbeat AI voice presets. Use the most natural/neutral available.

**Screen recordings:**
- Use real chat, real coach responses - not polished mockups
- Slight imperfections in the chat are trust signals, not problems
- The output document should look like a real working document, not a designed template

**Text graphics (split screen):**
- Font: bold sans-serif, white on black
- Animation: each line cuts in hard (no fade in) - creates urgency
- The arrow "→" before each output should appear 0.5 seconds after the pain line

**Color palette:**
- Dark background throughout (charcoal or near-black)
- White text
- One accent color for CTA button only (suggest deep blue or burnt orange - not green)
