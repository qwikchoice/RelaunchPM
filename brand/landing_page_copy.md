## SECTION 10: FAQ

**Q: Do I need to set anything up?**
No. Create an account, start a session, describe what's happening. The coach routes to the right mode automatically. First session takes 3 minutes to start.

**Q: What makes this different from ChatGPT?**
ChatGPT answers questions. This coach refuses to. It asks them back. It tracks your commitments, calls out your patterns, and pushes back when you're deciding from fear. It's a different use of the model.

**Q: What if I already have a job?**
The 5 use cases apply anywhere there's career uncertainty - offer decisions, skill gaps, overwhelming learning lists. You don't need to be laid off to use it.

**Q: How does the memory work?**
After every session, your state is saved. Next session opens with your last commitment: "Last time you said X. What happened?" You never repeat your background. Accountability compounds.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. No lock-in. No cancellation fees. If it stops being useful, cancel in 30 seconds.

**Q: Is my session data private?**
Yes. Your sessions are private to your account. Not used for training. Not shared.

## BRAND NOTES FOR FRAMER

**Colors:**
- Background: near-black (#0A0A0A or #111111)
- Primary text: white
- Accent: one strong color for CTA buttons only - suggest #E85D3F (burnt orange) or #2563EB (deep blue)
- Card backgrounds: dark gray (#1A1A1A)
- Avoid green (too "success-y" for the emotional tone)

**Typography:**
- Headline: bold, large, tight letter-spacing
- Body: clean sans-serif, generous line-height
- No script fonts. No decorative type.

**Photography/Visuals:**
- Real product screenshots over mockups
- No stock photos of people smiling at laptops
- Muted, real, honest visual tone

**Animation:**
- Subtle scroll-triggered fade-ins
- No aggressive animations or pop-ups
- Chat demo loop is the hero animation - let it do the work
